Sofia's Gymnastics competition 2022

Welcome to the 2022 Gymnastics competitions of the state of Queretaro...

The State Competition

The State of Queretaro had it's annual competition, where lots of contestants attended to earn a spot at the national competition that is taking place by the end of this year.

The event took place at the Queretaro 2000 park's olympic complex with the precence of multiple senior champions that acted as the jury.

Multiple gymnastic academies participated with dozens of competitors with the support of their families and coaches.

This is a compilation of Sofia's participation in the event.

Stages of the competition

  • Bars
  • Balance beam
  • Vault
  • Floor
